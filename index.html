Taniti

Welcome!

Welcome to Taniti, a small tropical island in the Pacific with an area of less than 500 square miles. You will be surprised by how much our home has to offer. Taniti's environment includes a small and safe harbor, lush tropical rainforest, and a mountainous interior, including a small active volcano, which will make great spots for sightseeing. The island is mostly flat land, making it easy to walk around and see everything, no matter how long your stay. As our guest, you will be delighted to experience all the fun activities, delightful lodging that undergoes regular reviews to uphold standards, and even if you're not too much of a walker, Taniti has a robust travel system to include accessible taxis in the city and rental cars wherever you may need.

For our new Travelers

Fear not if you're new to traveling, Taniti, we have included some answers common questions and concerns from previous guests.
  • Power outlets are 120 volts (Same as US)
  • Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • Drinking age is 18 and not strictly enforced
  • There is a central hospital with english speaking personel
  • Crime is rare in Taniti with only small reports of pickpocketing
  • Taniti uses the US dollar as currency
