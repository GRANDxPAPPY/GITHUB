Things to Do:

Most of our tourists come to Tanti to enjoy the beaches, but don't let that stop you from exploring our many other amazing wonders, including rainforest, our own volcano, museums, chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, and ziplining. dance clubs and helicopter rides. With all these events and activities, you can get a full scoop of Taniti culture, and it guarantees a new and wonderful adventure to make lifelong memories for you and your family every day.
